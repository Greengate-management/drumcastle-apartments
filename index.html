Suburban tranquility in the heart of Baltimore

Within the bustling city of Baltimore, you'll find the tranquil suburban lifestyle of Drumcastle Apartments. Our pet-friendly community offers spacious 2-bedroom homes with flowing layouts, modern appliances, and large closets.

Located conveniently near shopping, dining, transportation, and job opportunities, Drumcastle puts everything you need within reach — with the peace and quiet you've been looking for.
